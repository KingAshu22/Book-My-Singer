Biography -Krishna Pandey Live

Krishna Pandey, born in Ranchi, initially studied engineering at Anna University, Kerala, where he completed his B.Tech., and then worked at TCS for around 4 years. He found inspiration from and kept listening to artists like Lucky Ali, KK, and other artists like that while he was working at TCS in Kampala, Uganda, leading the project for Airtel. He left his job and came back to India to pursue his passion for music so he created Krishna pandey live band.

On his return, he started to learn from his Guru. a very established composer and session guitarist in the Bollywood industry, Mr. Raja Panda, and simultaneously started to perform, initially for small clubs and lounges, at Bandstand doing street shows with a lot of other upcoming and experienced artists, and later for private events, weddings, and college festivals in a lot of different kinds of events in the years to follow. He now has a full, power-packed 5 piece band along with other singers who he keeps collaborating with for shows, dancers, and other performers like solo artists and instrumentalists that he brings along at times to create the act.

He has more than 10 years of experience in the life performance industry, consisting of more than 500 shows in more than 12 countries like Dubai, Singapore, Malaysia, Cambodia, Bangkok, Uganda, South Africa, and many more
