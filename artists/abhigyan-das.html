Biography - Abhigyan Das

Abhigyan Das is a renowned Indian singer who rose to fame after participating in the 2016 edition of the popular singing reality show “Sa Re Ga Ma Pa”. He comes from Kolkata, West Bengal, and is well-known for his ability to sing various Indian musical genres, including classical, folk, and Bollywood. Abhigyan Das was one of the top contestants in Zee Sa Re Ga Ma Pa 2016. He had previously competed in the Zee Lil Champs and Mega Challenge in 2009, where he placed in the top six. He also won the Sa Re Ga Ma Pa Mega Challenge’s singer of the series award. He also took part in the LIL CHAMPS BENGAL competition and ended up winning the event.

Abhigyan Das started learning music at the age of five under the tutelage of his parents. For the past ten years and still today, he has been under the tutelage of Vidushi Tulika Ghosh. In 2010, he performed at the GIMA Awards. Additionally, he is the sibling of singer Shyamantan Das, who competed in Chhote Ustaad – Do Deshon Ki Ahwaz and won the series’ Singer of the Series award.Abhigyan Das’ brother is a trained singer as well.

Some of his favorite genres are Indian classical, western, Bollywood, Sufi, and folk. Apart from singing, Abhigyan can play the piano and guitar as well. Abhigyan’s versatility in singing different genres of music, along with his unique voice and style, garnered him widespread recognition. He has continued to pursue his passion for music by releasing several songs and music videos on different platforms and performing at various music concerts and events throughout the country. Abhigyan Das is a rising star in the Indian music industry, known for his talent and dedication towards music.
